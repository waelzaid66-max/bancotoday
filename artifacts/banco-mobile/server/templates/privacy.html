Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Overview

BANCO is an asset marketplace for cars, real estate, and industrial equipment. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have over it.

Data We Collect

When you create an account we collect your email address and the name and password you provide. When you contact a seller we record the listing, the action (call, WhatsApp, chat), and any name or phone number you choose to share. We also collect anonymous, app-activity signals (listings viewed, searches, taps) to personalize your feed.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to operate the marketplace: authenticating your account, syncing your saved listings across devices, connecting you with sellers, and improving recommendations. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising.

Camera & Photo Access

BANCO only requests access to your camera or photo library when you choose to add a photo (for example, your profile picture). We always explain why access is needed before the system prompt appears, and we never access your media in the background.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS, and stored data is encrypted at rest by our infrastructure providers. Access to personal data is restricted and logged.

Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account. Deletion anonymizes your account, removes your saved listings and activity history, erases personal contact details from your past inquiries, and removes your sign-in credentials from our authentication provider.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact privacy@banco.today.
